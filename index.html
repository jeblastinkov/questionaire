Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our claims process

How long will it take to process my claim?

Most auto insurance claims are processed within 7-10 business days after all required documentation has been received. Complex claims may take longer. You'll receive regular updates on the status of your claim.

What information do I need to file a claim?

To file a claim, you'll need:

  • Your policy number
  • Date, time, and location of the incident
  • Description of what happened
  • Vehicle information
  • Photos of the damage (if available)
  • Police report number (if applicable)
Will filing a claim increase my premium?

Not all claims result in premium increases. Factors that determine if your premium will increase include the type of claim, your driving history, and whether you were at fault. If you have accident forgiveness coverage, your first at-fault accident may not affect your premium.

Can I choose my own repair shop?

Yes, you have the right to choose your preferred repair shop. However, using one of our Allianz-approved repair shops can streamline the process, as we have direct billing arrangements with these facilities. All repairs from our network shops also come with a lifetime guarantee.

What if I'm not satisfied with the claim decision?

If you're not satisfied with the outcome of your claim, you can request a review by contacting your claims adjuster. If you're still not satisfied after the review, you can escalate to our Claims Resolution Team at 1-800-123-4567 or email claims.resolution@allianz.com.
